Available Helplines

Click on a helpline to call or visit their website for more information.

Mental Health Crisis

  • South African Suicide Crisis Line
    Call 0800 111 111
    Available 24/7 for suicide prevention and emotional support.
  • LifeLine South Africa
    Call 0861 324 357
    Confidential emotional support for anyone in distress.
  • SADAG (South African Depression and Anxiety Group)
    Call 0800 12 13 14
    Support for depression, anxiety, and mental health concerns.

Domestic Violence & Abuse

  • Domestic Violence Helpline
    Call 0800 150 150
    24/7 support for survivors of domestic abuse.
  • SAPS Crime Stop
    Call 08600 10111
    Report crime anonymously.

Substance Abuse Support

  • South African National Council on Alcoholism and Drug Dependence (SANCA)
    Call 0861 234 399
    Help for addiction and substance abuse.

International Helplines

  • International Suicide Prevention Hotline (USA)
    Call 988
    Nationwide 24/7 crisis lifeline.
  • International Disaster Emergency Service
    Visit IFRC Website

What to do in a crisis

If you are feeling overwhelmed or in immediate danger, try to:

  1. Find a safe place away from harm.
  2. Reach out to a trusted person or one of the helplines above.
  3. Take slow, deep breaths to help calm yourself.
  4. If you feel you are in immediate danger, call emergency services.

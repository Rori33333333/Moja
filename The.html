Types of Therapy

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Purpose: Restructure negative thought patterns and behaviors.

Common Uses: Anxiety, depression, OCD, phobias.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Purpose: Combines CBT with mindfulness to manage intense emotions.

Common Uses: Borderline personality disorder, self-harm, emotional regulation.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Purpose: Explore unconscious patterns rooted in past experiences.

Common Uses: Trauma, relationship issues, long-term emotional problems.

Humanistic Therapy

Purpose: Focus on self-growth and finding meaning.

Common Uses: Low self-esteem, self-exploration, grief.

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR)

Purpose: Reprocess trauma through guided eye movements.

Common Uses: PTSD, flashbacks, trauma.
